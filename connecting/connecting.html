Connecting

Beyond my work and academic projects, valuable experiences have come from connecting with people and exploring new perspectives. Here are a few networking opportunities, events, and conversations that allowed me to learn from people beyond my usual environment.

Event

mmk

As part of the Dean’s List, I had the chance to attend the Munich Management Colloquium (MMK) with a complimentary ticket. The event brought together leaders from industry and academia to discuss current challenges and future opportunities in areas such as innovation, digital transformation, and leadership. I am very thankful for this opportunity!

Networking

AI Value Creation with BCG

I also had the opportunity to participate in the course “AI Value Creation”, developed in partnership with BCG. Throughout the semester, we explored how companies can create meaningful value with AI, learning from both successful implementations and challenges. As a final project, we had the opportunity to present our work at the BCG office, followed by a networking session with BCG consultants, where we gained valuable insights into their projects, career paths, and the reality of consulting work.
