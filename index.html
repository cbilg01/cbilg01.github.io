Hello, I’m Catarina

Am I talkative? Most people who know me would probably say yes.

I have always been the person with a story to tell and a few thoughts to add. Over time, it became less about simply talking and more about connecting, finding new ways to see things, and creating something new from it. Funny enough, the one thing I have not always been good at is talking about my own work. So this space is my way of doing that: no small talk, just projects, ideas, experiences, and the chapters of my professional life as they are still being written.

Feel free to look around. Hope you enjoy it!
