Welcome to my life, work, and ideas

Hello, I’m Catarina

Can I present myself in chapters? Maybe not perfectly.

Unlike books, where chapters usually follow a clear order and build on one another, life has many chapters happening at the same time. They overlap, connect, and influence each other in different ways. Still, I like the idea of chapters. It feels like a simple way to look at different experiences and how they all come together into a larger story. That is why I chose this name for my website.

Here, I share a few chapters of my life, mostly from a professional point of view, while keeping the more personal ones to myself. Feel free to look around and explore my projects, work, and reflections. Hope you enjoy it!
