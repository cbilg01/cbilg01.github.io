University Project

PromoPack, an AI Prototype

In this project, our goal was to create a prototype of an AI-based business solution. Working in a group of five, mostly with people from a marketing background, we identified a common challenge in the daily work of marketing professionals: the creation of marketing assets, especially for technical industries, such as white papers, brochures, and product materials.

Based on this, we challenged ourselves to develop a tool that could automate part of the content creation process. The idea was simple: the user would upload a technical sheet and brand guidelines, select the type of asset they wanted to create, and receive a generated output adapted to the brand and product information.

Since none of us were advanced coders or came from a computer science background, developing the tool came with many challenges. However, after a lot of testing and problem-solving, we were able to build a working prototype. For me, it was very rewarding to see that we could create something functional with limited coding experience.

The prototype was not perfect. For example, the generated brochures were not easily editable and the design still had room for improvement. However, it was a strong starting point and showed the potential of using AI to support marketing workflows. This project also sparked my interest in understanding more about how LLMs work and how they can be applied in real business contexts.
