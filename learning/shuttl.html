University Project

SHUTTL by Munich Airport

For this university project, developed in partnership between Munich Airport, our group of three was challenged to create an innovative mobility solution that could attract more people to Munich Airport. Each group received a different mobility mode, and ours was bus mobility.

After researching the airport’s current mobility options, we identified a gap between low-cost but inconvenient public transport and fast but expensive options such as taxis or airport parking. Based on this, we created SHUTTL by Munich Airport, a premium, app-based, door-to-door e-shuttle service connecting selected Munich districts to MUC. The concept was designed to offer a more convenient, reliable, and affordable alternative for travelers, starting with a pilot in Schwabing-West and Maxvorstadt.

We developed the full business case, including the value proposition, operational model, revenue logic, implementation strategy, and target group, as well as a launch campaign. Our campaign focused on the key message: “Vacation starts at your doorstep.” It targeted urban, frequent millennial flyers who value convenience, predictability, and fair pricing. The launch campaign combined online and offline channels, including Instagram and Facebook ads, local influencer content, UGC through the #SkipTheDetour challenge, branded vehicles, and OOH banners in stations and streets. To bring the concept to life, we used AI to generate campaign assets such as branded shuttle visuals, social media posts, lifestyle scenes, and outdoor advertising mock-ups. Finally, we presented the concept and campaign to a representative from Munich Airport.

This project was especially interesting because we had to develop not only a marketing campaign, but also the entire mobility solution, almost like a start-up concept. Having classes near the airport, receiving input from Munich Airport, and working with them as a real project partner made the experience more practical and engaging. It taught me how to connect research, business strategy, mobility innovation, and AI-supported campaign creation into one coherent concept.
